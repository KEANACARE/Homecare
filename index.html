About Us

At Keana’s Home Care, we are more than just a care provider—we are a dedicated team committed to enhancing the quality of life for our clients and their families. Founded on the belief that everyone deserves to age with dignity and grace in the comfort of their own home, we provide compassionate, personalized support tailored to each individual’s needs.

Our journey began with a simple goal: to treat every client like a member of our own family. We understand the challenges of finding reliable, high-quality care, which is why we meticulously select and train our caregivers to ensure they meet the highest standards of professionalism and empathy.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote independence and enhance well-being by empowering individuals to live safely and confidently within the sanctuary of their own homes. We strive to provide exceptional, person-centered care that honors the unique life stories of our clients, fosters meaningful connections, and provides unwavering support to the families who trust us.

Why Choose Keana’s Home Care?

Choosing the right care is a big decision. Here is why families trust us:

  • Compassion-First Approach: We build relationships, not just tasks. Our caregivers are chosen for their big hearts and dedication.
  • Tailored Care Plans: We work closely with you to create a customized care schedule that fits your needs.
  • Trusted Professionals: Every member of our team undergoes a rigorous screening and background check.
  • Reliability & Consistency: We pride ourselves on being there when we say we will be with consistent communication.
  • Focus on Quality of Life: We promote engagement, social interaction, and activities that keep clients connected.

Welcome To Keana's Home Care

Our service differs from others in that we offer both a person-centered therapeutic approach based on wellness and compassionate support, together with bespoke practical life skills training. Our fully trained and experienced staff members specialize in supporting individuals on their journey to independence and aim to empower and support individuals through structured daily care.

Preparation for Independent Living

Keana's Home Care provides an essential service, supporting individuals as they progress to recovery together and with practical preparation for independent living by working collaboratively with families and professional services.

Accessing and supporting voluntary or paid work placements underpins our work to enable individuals to enter or re-enter the community. We are a new and innovative care provider where management has extensive experience in the care sector, with an excellent reputation for the range and quality of services offered. We provide high-quality, individualised care and support for adults.
