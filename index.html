About Us

At Keana’s Home Care, we are more than just a care provider—we are a dedicated team committed to enhancing the quality of life for our clients and their families. Founded on the belief that everyone deserves to age with dignity and grace in the comfort of their own home, we provide compassionate, personalized support tailored to each individual’s needs.

Our Staff

At Keana’s Home Care, we believe that the heart of exceptional care lies in the people who provide it. Our team is comprised of dedicated professionals who view caregiving not just as a profession, but as a calling. We understand that inviting someone into your home requires immense trust, which is why we go above and beyond to ensure our staff represents the highest standards of integrity, compassion, and clinical excellence.

Every member of our team undergoes a rigorous selection process, including comprehensive background checks and specialized training, ensuring that your loved ones feel safe, valued, and truly cared for every single day. To provide the most comprehensive support, our team includes a diverse range of specialists:

Registered Nurses (RN)

Our Registered Nurses are highly skilled healthcare leaders who hold advanced degrees and national licensure. They take a comprehensive approach to health management, which includes performing detailed patient assessments, designing customized care plans, and safely administering medications. Their expertise ensures that every client receives high-level clinical oversight and professional medical support within the comfort of their own home.

Practical Nurses (PN)

Our Licensed Practical Nurses provide essential, hands-on nursing care and have completed rigorous formal training and certification programs. They are the backbone of daily health monitoring, assisting with vital signs, wound care, and basic medical procedures. With a focus on both physical health and patient comfort, our PNs ensure that daily medical needs are met with precision and a gentle touch.

Professional Housekeepers

We believe a clean environment is vital for health and peace of mind. Our housekeeping staff is dedicated to maintaining a pristine, sanitary, and organized living space for our clients. From routine cleaning to ensuring common areas are tidy and welcoming, they handle the heavy lifting of home maintenance so that our clients can focus entirely on their recovery and well-being.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Keana’s Home Care offer?
We offer Registered Nursing (RN), Practical Nursing (PN), and professional Housekeeping services tailored to your specific needs.
Are your caregivers trained and certified?
Yes. All staff undergo rigorous screening, enhanced DBS checks, and specialized training to maintain high-level clinical excellence.
What payment options do you accept?
We accept Bank Transfers, Western Union, MoneyGram, Remitly, and more for your convenience.
Can I change my care schedule?
Yes. We offer flexible plans that can be adjusted as your needs evolve. Just contact our team to update your schedule.
Will we always have the same caregiver?
We aim to assign a primary caregiver to each client to foster trust, with a backup team for holiday or illness coverage.
How quickly can care services begin?
We can often arrange an initial assessment within 24–48 hours. Services can often begin almost immediately after.
